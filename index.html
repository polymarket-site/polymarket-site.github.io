FAQ

Polymarket questions

01What is Polymarket?

Polymarket is a prediction market experience for taking positions on event outcomes. This page frames the wallet and USDC flow so you can prepare funds, review route details and open the app with a clearer plan.

02How does the Polymarket app flow work?

Connect a wallet, choose the USDC route, review the live quote and sign only after the wallet prompt matches your intended action. A wallet connection keeps the approval step separate from the page.

03Which chains and assets are supported?

The app focuses on USDC and market-ready wallet balances. Route availability can change by network, so check the supported chains page and the live app before signing.

04How long does a Polymarket route take?

Timing depends on the selected network, wallet confirmation and current chain conditions. The estimated completion window is shown in the app before you approve anything.

05What does it cost to use Polymarket?

Costs depend on the route, network conditions and any market action you choose. The app displays the live quote and network fee information before you sign, so avoid relying on stale screenshots or copied instructions.

06How should I use Polymarket safely?

Verify the site, inspect every wallet prompt, confirm the asset and network, and remember that prediction markets can lose value. Do not sign approvals you do not understand.
